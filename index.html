じゃんけん

じゃんけん or じゃん拳 [janken] - is the Japanese equivalent of Rock Paper Scissors.

In janken, we use グー[guu] for rock, パー[paa] for paper, and チョキ[choki] for scissors.

It plays more or less the same as the English version though, good luck!
